4 Types 

1.	Supervised machine learning
- Regression
- Classification
2.	Unsupervised machine learning
- Clustering
- Dimensionality reduction
- Anomaly detection
3.	Semi-supervised machine learning
4.	Reinforcement

Real-Life applications:

1.	Supervised :

⦁	Fraud Detection: Identifying fraudulent transactions in banking.
⦁	Medical Diagnosis: Predicting diseases like Parkinson's or cancer based on patient data.
⦁	Customer Churn Prediction: Anticipating if a customer is likely to stop using a service.
⦁	Image and Speech Recognition: Classifying objects in images or transcribing spoken words.
⦁	Spam Detection: Filtering unwanted emails.

2. Unsupervised : 

⦁	Recommendation Systems – Clustering users/items to suggest similar products or content.
⦁	Image Segmentation – Separating objects or regions in medical and satellite images.
⦁	Document Clustering – Grouping news articles, legal documents, or emails by topic.
⦁	Social Network Analysis – Identifying communities or influencer groups.

3. Reinforcement : 

⦁	Robotics – Autonomous navigation, grasping, and motion control
⦁	Self-Driving Vehicles – Lane keeping, adaptive cruise control, decision-making
⦁	Gaming – AlphaGo, game AI strategy optimization
⦁	Finance – Algorithmic trading, portfolio optimization, risk management
⦁	Healthcare – Treatment planning, dosage optimization, clinical decision support
⦁	Supply Chain & Logistics – Inventory control, route optimization, demand forecasting
⦁	Energy Management – Smart grids, energy consumption optimization
